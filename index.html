Chameleons change the color of their skin to blend in with their surroundings. They can become any color that they want. Chameleons are reptiles. Some chameleons only have a life-span of 7 years, while others up to 85 years!

Camels have humps on their backs. They can run up to 40 mph, and also have a lifespan of around 40 years. Camels almost way a ton. Camels are like the horses of deserts.
